Idlescape augment calculator

You can currently use this calculator to find the expected costs of augmenting an item to a certain level, or calculate how much xp you'll get on average from augmenting a certain item.
You can put numbers or simple math expressions into the fields, and use 'k' or 'm' shorthands - try putting 15*1.2k to calculate the cost of 15 ingots worth 1200 each.
You can mouse-over some fields to get a clarification of what they need.

Limitations:

Xp calculations do not take into account leveling up while augmenting, which slightly increases success chances.
For xp calculations, only augments up to +50 are considered.
Remember that in practice you'll usually get less than average xp; this is offset by gaining a lot on rare, high-level augments.
